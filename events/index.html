CODE STORM

Code Storm Do you like to code? Do you like to be the best among all your peers when it comes to coding? If you do, then this is your opportunity to put your coding skills to the test and prove your worth! The event will take place as a series of 2 rounds which are described below:-
Round 1 : This round consists of a written test which would test your knowledge on basic programming concepts. A total of 10 teams would be shortlisted from this round.
Round 2 : This round consists of a series of competitive coding questions of varying difficulties. The teams will be evaluated based on their successful test cases and the time taken to solve the problem statements.

SUMMIT

SUMMIT IEEE UVCE's flagship event Summit has been one of the most interesting events till date and it's now entering the arena with a better version! Summit is a student parliament where participants present their ideas and views on a certain topic, to the judges and audience. There will be a panel discussion amongst the renowned panellists and the moderator prior to the rounds of event. There are two rounds and they are as described below.
ROUND 1: The participants would be given a problem statement and are expected to come up with interesting solutions and present their ideas along with a ppt. The best ones would make it to the final round.
ROUND 2: This would be the final round of the event which is of similar format but with a more challenging problem statement. Judgement would be based on various criteria which would be disclosed later. The students would also be given a problem statement on spot and they are required to present their unique solution for the same without a ppt.

Team size - 2 or 3
Cost of ticket - Free entry

LAZER TAG

Lazer Tag is a fun event where you get to experience a "First Person Shooter" in real life . Each participant will be given a laser gun and a body armour. The participants will have to form a team and compete against the other team. The participants can use the help of obstacles to evade from their opponents and shoot the opponents to gain points for their respective team. At the end of the allotted time, the team with the most number of "kills" wins.
